Bo-Katan Kyrze

Bo-Katan Kryze was a Mandalorian human female princess born into House Kryze and was the Mand'alor during the New Republic Era. Over her life, she became a lieutenant in the Death Watch terrorist group, the commanding officer of its Nite Owls subdivision, the leader of the Mandalore resistance, and even laid claim over all of her homeworld, the planet Mandalore.
